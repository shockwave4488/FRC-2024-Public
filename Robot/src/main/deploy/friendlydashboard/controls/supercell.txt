Driver:
 - Left Joystick: Translation
 - Right Joystick: Rotation
 - Start Button: Toggle between field relative (default) and robot relative
 - Right Trigger: Lock wheels
 - Left Trigger: Hold for half speed
 - Right Bumper: Rotate around front of robot
 - Left Bumper: Cancel auto score
 - A (Cross): Drive to charge station & auto balance
 - B (Circle): Auto balance
 - Y (Triangle): Launch cube (same as button box 14)

Button Box:
1. Move arm to piece pickup
2. Move arm to substation
3. Auto score mid right
4. Auto score top right
5. Move arm to low score
6. Auto score mid center
7. Auto score top center
8. Cube intake
9. Cone intake
10. Auto score mid left
11. Auto score top left
12. Purge cube
13. Purge cone
14. Launch cube (same as driver Y)
15. Lower arm offset
16. Raise arm offset
