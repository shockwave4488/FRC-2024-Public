Driver:
 - Left Joystick: Translation
 - Right Joystick: Rotation
 - Back: Toggle between field relative (default) and robot relative
 - Right Trigger: Colorless intake
 - Left Trigger: Purge back
 - Right Bumper: Shoot
 - Left Bumper: Purge forward
 - X (Square): Turn to hub
 - A (Cross): Drive to charge station & auto balance (from Supercell)
 - POV Up: Increase shooter RPM offset
 - POV Down: Decrease shooter RPM offset

Button Box:
1. Vision align
3. Shoot
4. Limelight snapshot
5. Launch fender shot
6. Charge shooter
7. Decrease shooter RPM offset
8. Increase shooter RPM offset
10. Purge back
11. Purge forward
14. Color intake (no purge)
15. Colorless intake
16. Color intake (purge)
